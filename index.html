Myself

My decision in pursuing a Physics masters degree at UCL was mainly based on a love for mathematics. The fact that natural phenomena could be explained and predicted in a mathematical manner was extremely intriguing. However, to me, the limitations of simply using maths quickly became apparent - it lacked the visuals. Simulations of equations were much easier to understand than the equation on paper. My journey in software development began by simulating bacteria growth under the effects antibiotics in MATLAB, and ever since, I've realised I simply enjoy coding, especially when there's maths involved.
